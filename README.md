# FL02 - Advanced TMA Extremes

## Overview
**FL02** is an advanced variation of the Triangular Moving Average (TMA) Bands system. Designed to identify deep extreme market conditions, FL02 provides a wider market envelope (default ATR multiplier of 3.0) to highlight peak overbought and oversold zones. With a clean visual approach that hides the center channel line, it ensures an uncluttered chart while keeping focus purely on critical dynamic reversal levels.

## Key Features
- **Wide Extreme TMA Bands:** Uses expanded ATR multipliers tailored for identifying prime market reversal zones and exhaustion points.
- **Clean Interface design:** Focuses completely on the outer dynamic support and resistance levels, removing the midline to maintain a tidy workspace.
- **Multi-Timeframe (MTF) Support:** Projects robust higher timeframe dynamic channels seamlessly onto lower timeframes for top-down analysis.
- **Advanced Alert System:** Features customizable alerts (Popup, Sound, Email), capable of tracking breaches on closed or live current candles.

## How to Install
1. Download the compiled files (`.ex4` for MT4 or `.ex5` for MT5) from this repository.
2. Open your MetaTrader platform.
3. Click on `File` -> `Open Data Folder` in the top menu.
4. For MT4, navigate to the `MQL4/Indicators` folder. For MT5, navigate to the `MQL5/Indicators` folder.
5. Paste the downloaded files into the folder.
6. Refresh the "Navigator" panel in MetaTrader, or simply restart the platform.
7. Find the indicator in the Navigator, drag and drop it onto your desired chart, and start trading!
